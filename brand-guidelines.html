Brief

I wanted to create something that wouldn't look out of place in an S&P 500 annual report. The kind of document where every decision is deliberate and nothing is there by accident.

This is the pinnacle of minimal, premium design. One typeface, a palette you could count on one hand, and rules clear enough that a supplier around the corner and a customer on the other side of the world can both pick it up cold and know exactly what to do with it.
